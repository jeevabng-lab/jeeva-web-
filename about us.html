About Jeeva

Jeeva is a non-profit organization committed to environmental education and empowering communities to live sustainably.

Our Mission

To educate, inspire, and empower individuals and communities towards environmental stewardship and sustainable living.

Our Vision

A world where communities thrive in harmony with nature, preserving the planet for future generations.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We act with honesty and transparency in all we do.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of working together.
  • Respect: We honor the earth and all living beings.
  • Innovation: We embrace creative solutions for sustainability challenges.
